# OL-SYS-002 Mining System

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Owner: OreLabs
Category: Gameplay System

---

# Purpose

The Mining System is the primary gameplay activity of OreLabs.

Mining transforms the player's Strength into resources, progression and meaningful decisions.

Mining is not a repetitive task between upgrades—it is one of the core gameplay experiences that remains engaging throughout the entire game.

---

# Design Philosophy

Mining is the heart of gameplay.

The objective is not to remove mining through progression, but to make it increasingly more interesting.

As players become stronger, they gain more choices rather than simply making every Ore trivial.

Progression should expand possibilities instead of eliminating challenge.

---

# Player Experience

The player should constantly feel:

"I've become stronger."

"I finally unlocked this Pickaxe."

"I can now choose how I want to farm."

"Should I farm fast or farm valuable resources?"

"I found something rare!"

Mining should continuously reward both progression and decision-making.

---

# Core Gameplay Loop

1. Explore the world.
2. Find an Ore.
3. Mine the Ore.
4. Receive Resources while mining.
5. Destroy the Ore.
6. Collect the remaining rewards.
7. Continue exploring.
8. Sell Resources.
9. Upgrade progression.
10. Return to mining.

---

# Ore Interaction

Every Ore has Health.

Mining gradually reduces Ore Health.

Resources are rewarded progressively while the Ore is being mined.

Destroying the Ore grants the final reward before the Ore eventually respawns.

Mining should provide continuous visual and gameplay feedback.

---

# Mining Damage

Mining Damage is determined primarily by the equipped Pickaxe.

Strength does not directly increase Mining Damage.

Instead:

Strength

↓

Unlocks stronger Pickaxes

↓

Better Pickaxes improve mining efficiency

↓

Mining unlocks better resources

This keeps Strength as the root progression system while allowing Pickaxes to define mining performance.

---

# Strategic Mining

Mining should always involve player choice.

There should rarely be one objectively best Ore to mine.

Players constantly choose between:

- Fast farming
- Valuable Ores
- Rare resources
- Gem farming
- Exploration
- Progression efficiency

The best decision depends on the player's current goals.

Mining is a strategic activity rather than repetitive clicking.

---

# Rare Ore Variants

Occasionally, larger or rarer Ore variants appear throughout the world.

Rare Ores provide:

- More Resources
- Increased Gem chance
- Higher overall value
- Greater visual excitement

Rare Ores are intended to create memorable gameplay moments and encourage exploration.

---

# Progressive Rewards

Players receive Resources while mining instead of only after destroying the Ore.

This creates:

- Constant feedback
- Frequent rewards
- Better visual satisfaction
- More engaging gameplay

Large rewards remain rare, while small rewards happen consistently.

---

# Respawn

Destroyed Ores respawn after a short delay.

The world should always contain enough available Ores to encourage movement and exploration.

Players should naturally travel across the map instead of waiting for a single Ore to respawn.

---

# Relationship with Other Systems

Depends on:

- Strength System
- Pickaxe System
- Ore System

Supports:

- Economy
- Backpack
- Gems
- Pets
- Rebirth
- Crafting

---

# Design Rules

Mining must always feel responsive.

Mining must always provide visual feedback.

Mining must encourage exploration.

Mining must reward player decisions.

Progression should make mining broader, not trivial.

A better Pickaxe expands mining possibilities rather than removes mining challenge.

---

# Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Critical Hits
- Mining Buffs
- Ore Mutations
- Giant Ore Veins
- World Events
- Cooperative Mining
- Area Bonuses

---

# Success Criteria

The Mining System is successful when:

- Mining remains enjoyable throughout the entire game.
- Players regularly make meaningful farming decisions.
- Different mining strategies remain viable.
- Better Pickaxes create new opportunities rather than removing gameplay.
- Mining supports every major progression system in OreLabs.

---

# OreLabs Mining Principle

> Mining is the core gameplay experience of OreLabs.

> Progression should never remove mining.

> Progression should continuously create new and more interesting ways to mine.

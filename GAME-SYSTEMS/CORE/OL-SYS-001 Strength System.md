# OL-SYS-001 Strength System

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Owner: OreLabs
Category: Gameplay System

---

# Purpose

The Strength System is the foundation of the entire progression system in OreLabs.

Strength is not simply a statistic.

Strength represents the player's overall progression and determines access to new stages of the game.

Every major gameplay system either depends on Strength or helps the player gain it more efficiently.

---

# Design Philosophy

Players are not training a number.

Players are unlocking the next level of the game.

Every increase in Strength should feel meaningful because it brings the player closer to:

- Better Pickaxes
- Better Ores
- Better Progression
- New Areas
- New Opportunities

Strength is the root of progression.

Mining begins with Strength.

---

# Player Experience

The player should constantly think:

"I only need a little more Strength."

"Now I can finally buy that Pickaxe."

"I've become stronger."

"I'm ready for the next stage."

Strength should always feel valuable.

---

# Core Gameplay Loop

1. Train Strength.
2. Unlock a better Pickaxe.
3. Mine better Ores.
4. Sell Resources.
5. Earn Coins.
6. Buy better Training Equipment.
7. Train more Strength.
8. Repeat until Rebirth.

---

# Sources of Strength

Primary source:

- Training Equipment (Dumbbells)

Future sources may include:

- Pets
- Temporary Buffs
- Events
- Multipliers
- Special Systems

These systems improve Strength gain but never replace the training process.

---

# Strength Usage

Strength is used to unlock:

- Pickaxes
- Progression Milestones
- Future Gameplay Systems

Strength is never consumed.

It permanently represents player progression until Rebirth.

---

# Relationship with Other Systems

Strength directly influences:

- Pickaxe System
- Mining System
- Progression System
- Rebirth System

Other systems may increase Strength gain efficiency.

---

# Progression Rules

Every new Training Equipment provides faster Strength growth.

Higher Strength unlocks stronger Pickaxes.

Stronger Pickaxes unlock stronger Ores.

Progression always starts with Strength.

---

# Rebirth Interaction

After every Rebirth:

- Strength resets.
- The player begins progression again.
- Global multipliers increase future progression.
- New progression tiers become available.

Rebirth is not a punishment.

It is the gateway to the next stage of the game.

---

# Character Growth

As Strength increases:

- The player's character gradually becomes larger.
- Growth should feel smooth and natural.
- The visual change communicates progression.

After Rebirth:

- Character size returns to its initial state.
- Growth begins again.

The system should remain simple and readable.

---

# Design Rules

Strength must always have value.

Strength must always unlock something meaningful.

Strength must never become a meaningless number.

Every point of Strength should move the player closer to new gameplay.

---

# Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Strength Multipliers
- Temporary Boosts
- New Training Equipment
- World-specific Training Methods
- Passive Strength Bonuses

---

# Success Criteria

The Strength System is successful when:

- Players always want more Strength.
- Strength naturally drives progression.
- Every unlocked Pickaxe feels earned.
- Character growth reinforces progression visually.
- Strength remains the foundation of the entire game.

# OL-SYS-010 Titles System

## Purpose

The Titles System rewards player achievements, dedication and exploration.

Titles primarily represent prestige and identity while providing small gameplay bonuses.

Players should feel proud to display their accomplishments.

---

## Core Philosophy

Titles are earned.

They represent milestones rather than progression requirements.

A Title should first tell a story about the player.

Its bonus is a secondary reward.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I earned this.
- This Title shows what I accomplished.
- Other players know what I achieved.
- I want to collect more Titles.

Titles should become long-term collectibles.

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Complete an objective.
2. Unlock a new Title.
3. Equip the Title.
4. Receive a small passive bonus.
5. Continue progressing.
6. Unlock even rarer Titles.

---

## Title Sources

Titles may be obtained from:

- Rebirth milestones
- Quests
- Hidden secrets
- Exploration
- Gem merchants
- Limited-time events
- Future achievements
- Premium purchases (cosmetic-focused)

Every acquisition method should feel meaningful.

---

## Passive Bonuses

Titles provide small passive bonuses.

Examples include:

- Coin Bonus
- Strength Bonus
- Luck Bonus
- Gem Bonus
- Mining Damage Bonus
- Mining Speed Bonus

Some Titles may provide no gameplay bonus and exist purely for prestige.

Titles should never become the player's primary source of power.

---

## Equipment

Players may own many Titles.

Only one Title can be equipped at a time.

Choosing a Title becomes another small strategic decision.

---

## Visual Identity

Every Title should have its own visual presentation.

Possible features:

- Unique colors
- Animated text
- Background effects
- Particle effects
- Special borders
- Future visual upgrades

Higher rarity Titles should immediately stand out.

---

## Relationship with Other Systems

Depends on:

- Rebirth
- Quests
- Exploration
- Economy

Supports:

- Long-term progression
- Player identity
- Collection
- Prestige

---

## Design Rules

- Titles should always feel rewarding.
- Titles should never replace progression systems.
- Bonuses should remain relatively small.
- Prestige is more important than power.
- Rare Titles should feel memorable.

---

## Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Animated Titles
- Seasonal Titles
- Guild Titles
- Competitive Titles
- Hidden Titles
- Collection Rewards

---

## Success Criteria

The Titles System is successful when:

- Players enjoy collecting Titles.
- Rare Titles become status symbols.
- Bonuses remain balanced.
- Players feel proud to display their achievements.
- Titles support progression without dominating it.

---

## Title Principle

> Titles represent achievements first.

> Power is only a small reward for the journey.

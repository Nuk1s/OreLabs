# OL-SYS-011 AFK System

## Purpose

The AFK System allows players to continue making limited progress while they are away from the game.

AFK systems are designed to support progression, not replace active gameplay.

Active play should always remain the fastest and most rewarding way to progress.

---

## Core Philosophy

AFK is a convenience feature.

It exists to reduce downtime for players who cannot actively play.

AFK should never outperform manual gameplay.

The player should always feel rewarded for actively playing the game.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I can still make some progress while I'm away.
- Active gameplay is still much better.
- AFK helps me, but it doesn't play the game for me.

---

## AFK Strength Training

Players can enable automatic Strength training.

The character automatically trains Strength without repeated player input.

AFK Strength gains are intentionally slower than active training.

---

## AFK Egg Opening

Players can automatically open Eggs.

The system continues opening Eggs while the player has enough Gems.

This system is designed for long collection sessions and searching for rare Pets.

---

## Future AFK Mining

Future updates may introduce AFK Mining.

The character automatically moves between nearby Ores and mines them.

AFK Mining intentionally has reduced efficiency compared to manual mining.

Possible limitations:

- Slower movement
- Slower mining speed
- No intelligent path optimization
- No strategic ore selection

Manual Mining should always remain more profitable.

---

## Future AFK Features

Possible future additions:

- AFK Backpack Selling
- AFK Quest Progress
- AFK Event Participation
- Smart AFK Priorities

Only if they preserve the advantage of active gameplay.

---

## Relationship with Other Systems

Depends on:

- Strength System
- Mining System
- Pet System
- Gem System

Supports:

- Long-term Progression
- Player Retention
- Convenience

---

## Design Rules

- Active gameplay must always be superior.
- AFK exists for convenience.
- AFK must never replace player decisions.
- AFK should preserve progression without trivializing gameplay.
- Every AFK feature should have reduced efficiency.

---

## Success Criteria

The AFK System is successful when:

- Players appreciate making progress while away.
- Active players remain significantly more efficient.
- AFK encourages players to return instead of replacing gameplay.
- AFK improves retention without damaging game balance.

---

## AFK Principle

> AFK preserves progression.

> Active gameplay creates progression.

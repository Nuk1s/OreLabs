# OL-SYS-007 Pet System

## Purpose

The Pet System provides long-term progression through specialization.

Pets are designed to enhance different playstyles rather than replace core gameplay.

They reward persistence, luck, and strategic decision-making.

---

## Core Philosophy

Pets are not the player's power.

Pets improve the player's strengths.

The strongest player is not the one with the rarest Pet, but the one who builds the best combination for their current objective.

Pets exist to create strategic variety, collection motivation, and long-term excitement.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I finally got a rare Pet.
- This Pet perfectly fits my build.
- I want to collect more Pets.
- Different situations require different Pet teams.
- Rare Pets are exciting because they change how I play.

Obtaining a rare Pet should always be a memorable moment.

---

## Pet Progression Philosophy

Pets have two different progression stages.

### Early Game Pets

Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Epic Pets primarily increase basic progression.

They usually improve:

- Strength Gain
- Coins
- Mining Efficiency
- Basic Progression Speed

Their purpose is to accelerate the player's early and mid-game development.

---

### Late Game Pets

Legendary, Mythic, and Secret Pets introduce unique passive abilities.

These abilities are designed to change the player's strategy rather than simply increase numbers.

Examples include:

- Gem Drop Chance
- Mining Luck
- Critical Chance
- Critical Damage
- Rare Ore Chance
- Mining Speed
- Resource Multiplier
- Future Passive Effects

Late Game Pets should feel special because they unlock new playstyles.

---

## Pet Identity

Every Secret Pet should have its own identity.

Secret Pets are not just stronger versions of existing Pets.

Instead, they should be specialists.

Examples:

- a Secret Pet focused on Gems
- a Secret Pet focused on Speed
- a Secret Pet focused on Luck
- a Secret Pet focused on Strength
- a Secret Pet focused on Critical Chance

Secret Pets should create reasons to return to older Eggs even after the player has progressed further.

---

## Collection Philosophy

Players should continue returning to older Eggs if those Eggs contain unique Pets with valuable passive abilities.

Older Pets should remain useful if their effect supports a specific strategy.

No Secret Pet should become completely obsolete simply because a new Egg exists.

This creates long-term collection value instead of linear replacement.

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Collect Gems.
2. Open Eggs.
3. Obtain Pets.
4. Build a Pet Team.
5. Receive passive bonuses.
6. Improve progression.
7. Return to collecting better or more specialized Pets.

---

## Pet Roles

Pets provide passive bonuses.

Examples include:

- Strength Gain
- Coin Multiplier
- Gem Multiplier
- Mining Luck
- Critical Chance
- Critical Damage
- Mining Damage
- Rare Drop Chance
- Future Gameplay Bonuses

Different Pets should encourage different builds.

---

## Team Building

Players should choose Pets based on their current goals.

Example strategies:

- Strength Training Team
- Coin Farming Team
- Gem Farming Team
- Mining Team
- Luck Team
- Specialized Event Team

Changing Pets should feel like changing strategy rather than simply increasing numbers.

---

## Progression

Players naturally progress by:

- Unlocking better Eggs.
- Finding rarer Pets.
- Building stronger combinations.
- Optimizing different Pet teams.

Progression should remain exciting throughout the game.

---

## Relationship with Other Systems

Depends on:

- Gem System
- Egg System

Supports:

- Strength
- Mining
- Economy
- Progression
- Future Systems

---

## Design Rules

- Every Pet should have a purpose.
- Different Pets should encourage different strategies.
- Pets should complement gameplay, not replace it.
- Rare Pets should feel memorable.
- Players should always want to improve their collection.
- Stronger Pets should increase progression.
- Rarer Pets should change progression.

---

## Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Pet Fusion
- Pet Evolution
- Pet Levels
- Secret Pets
- Seasonal Pets
- Event Pets
- Passive Pet Skills

---

## Success Criteria

The Pet System is successful when:

- Players become excited when opening Eggs.
- Different Pet builds remain viable.
- Rare Pets create memorable moments.
- Players continue collecting Pets throughout progression.
- Pets support gameplay without becoming mandatory.
- Older Eggs remain relevant because of unique Pet effects.

---

## Pet Principle

> Pets do not define the player.

> They define how the player chooses to play.

# OL-SYS-008 Rebirth System

## Purpose

The Rebirth System divides the game into progression chapters.

Rebirth is not a reset in the negative sense.

Rebirth is a milestone that marks the completion of one chapter and the beginning of the next.

It transforms progression into a long-term journey with meaningful stages.

---

## Core Philosophy

Rebirth is the bridge between chapters.

The player should reach a natural limit in the current stage of progression, then use Rebirth to enter the next stage with stronger long-term bonuses.

Rebirth should feel like advancement, not punishment.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I have completed this chapter.
- I am ready for the next stage.
- My progress was meaningful.
- I am stronger for the next run.
- New content is now available.

Rebirth should feel exciting, not exhausting.

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Progress through the current chapter.
2. Reach the current progression limit.
3. Perform Rebirth.
4. Receive permanent bonuses.
5. Unlock a new chapter.
6. Continue progression with stronger long-term growth.

---

## Chapter Structure

Each chapter represents a progression stage of the game.

A chapter may include:

- New Areas
- New Ores
- New Pickaxes
- New Pets
- New Goals
- New Progression Opportunities

The game should feel larger with every chapter.

---

## Rebirth Rewards

Rebirth provides permanent progression benefits.

Possible effects include:

- Strength multipliers
- Coin multipliers
- Better progression efficiency
- Access to new content
- Access to new systems

Rebirth rewards should always feel valuable.

---

## Progression Gate

Each chapter should have a meaningful end point.

The player should naturally feel that the current chapter has been mastered before moving on.

Rebirth exists to turn mastery into a new beginning.

---

## Design Rules

- Rebirth must always feel meaningful.
- Rebirth should unlock new possibilities.
- Rebirth should never feel like wasted progress.
- Rebirth should create a clear sense of chapter completion.
- Rebirth should preserve the value of earlier progression.
- Rebirth must support long-term replayability.

---

## Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Rebirth Milestones
- Rebirth Rewards Trees
- Rebirth-Specific Perks
- Chapter Bonuses
- Rebirth Visual Effects
- Special Rebirth UI
- Rebirth Challenges

---

## Success Criteria

The Rebirth System is successful when:

- Players understand why they should rebirth.
- Rebirth feels like a real milestone.
- New chapters feel exciting.
- Older chapters remain meaningful.
- Rebirth strengthens long-term progression.

---

## Rebirth Principle

> Rebirth is not a loss.

> Rebirth is the transition from one chapter of progression to the next.

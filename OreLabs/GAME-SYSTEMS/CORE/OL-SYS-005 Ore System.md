# OL-SYS-005 Ore System

## Purpose

The Ore System defines every mineable resource in OreLabs.

Ores are the primary source of progression, economy and exploration.

Each new Ore represents a new stage of the player's journey.

---

## Core Philosophy

Ores should not only differ by value.

Each Ore should feel like a meaningful progression milestone.

Unlocking a new Ore should always feel exciting.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I discovered something new.
- This Ore is more valuable.
- My Pickaxe is finally strong enough.
- I want to see what's in the next area.

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Unlock a new area.
2. Discover a new Ore.
3. Mine the Ore.
4. Collect Resources.
5. Sell Resources.
6. Improve progression.
7. Discover the next Ore.

---

## Ore Properties

Every Ore has:

- Health
- Coin Value
- Gem Drop Chance
- Respawn Time
- Visual Appearance

Future properties may include:

- Rarity
- Special Effects
- Event Variants

---

## Progression

Each new Ore should generally provide:

- Higher value
- Higher Health
- Better progression opportunities

New Ores should feel rewarding without making previous Ores useless.

---

## Rare Variants

Some Ores may spawn as rare variants.

Rare variants provide:

- More Resources
- Higher Coin rewards
- Increased Gem chance
- Better visual effects

Rare variants create memorable gameplay moments.

---

## Exploration

New Ores should encourage exploration.

Players should naturally move through the world searching for better resources instead of staying in one place.

---

## Relationship with Other Systems

Depends on:

- Mining System
- Pickaxe System

Supports:

- Economy
- Gems
- Backpack
- Progression
- Rebirth

---

## Design Rules

- Every Ore must feel unique.
- Every Ore should have a purpose.
- New Ores should expand progression.
- Old Ores should remain useful.
- Ore progression must remain easy to understand.

---

## Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Ore Families
- Mutated Ores
- Event Ores
- Legendary Ores
- Seasonal Ores
- World-specific Ores

---

## Success Criteria

The Ore System is successful when:

- Players are excited to discover new Ores.
- Ore progression feels natural.
- Different Ores remain useful throughout progression.
- Exploration is encouraged.
- Ores support the game's economy and progression.

---

## Ore Principle

> Every new Ore should feel like discovering a new opportunity, not simply finding a more expensive rock.

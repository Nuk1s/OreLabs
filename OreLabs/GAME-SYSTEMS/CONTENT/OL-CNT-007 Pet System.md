# OL-CNT-007 Pet System

## Purpose

The Pet System provides long-term progression through collectible companions.

Pets allow players to specialize their builds, improve progression and create different gameplay strategies.

Collecting Pets should remain exciting throughout the entire game.

---

## Core Philosophy

Pets are not simply stronger versions of previous Pets.

Different Pets specialize in different bonuses.

Players should build teams based on their current goals.

Collecting, upgrading and optimizing Pets is one of the game's primary long-term progression systems.

---

## Player Experience

The player should feel:

- I finally found the Pet I wanted.
- My team became stronger.
- I want to optimize my build.
- I have a reason to return to older Eggs.
- I want to complete my collection.

---

# Pet Inventory

The inventory allows players to:

- Equip Pets
- Unequip Pets
- Delete Pets
- Lock Pets
- View Pet statistics
- Search Pets
- Sort Pets
- Filter Pets

Built-in buttons:

- Equip Best
- Unequip All

The inventory should remain clean and easy to manage.

---

# Equip Best

Equip Best supports intelligent filtering.

Before equipping Pets, players may choose which bonus to prioritize.

Examples:

- Coins
- Strength
- Gems
- Gem Chance
- Luck
- Mining Speed
- Mining Damage
- Egg Luck
- Movement Speed
- Future bonuses

The system automatically equips the strongest available team for the selected attribute.

---

# Pet Teams

Players may create multiple saved Pet Teams.

Each team stores:

- selected Pets
- team name

Example:

Team 1 — Coins

Team 2 — Strength

Team 3 — Mining

Team 4 — Gems

Team 5 — Eggs

Players may instantly switch between saved teams.

---

# Team Hotkeys

Players may assign hotkeys to saved Pet Teams.

Pressing the assigned key immediately switches to the corresponding team.

This allows players to change builds without opening the inventory.

---

# Pet Rarities

Possible rarities:

- Common
- Uncommon
- Rare
- Epic
- Legendary
- Mythic
- Secret

Not every Egg must contain every rarity.

Each Egg defines its own Pet pool.

# Passive Bonuses

Pets may provide many different passive bonuses.

Examples include:

- Coins
- Strength
- Gems
- Gem Chance
- Luck
- Egg Luck
- Mining Speed
- Mining Damage
- Movement Speed
- Future bonuses

Higher rarity Pets generally provide:

- stronger bonuses;
- more passive abilities;
- more specialized builds.

---

# Secret Pets

Secret Pets are a separate Pet class.

They feature:

- unique appearance;
- larger visual size;
- multiple passive bonuses;
- unique animations;
- exclusive visual effects.

Secret Pets should immediately stand out.

# Design Rules

- Every Pet should have value.
- Different builds should remain viable.
- Secret Pets should feel truly special.
- Higher rarity Pets should become more complex.
- Team switching should be instant.
- Inventory management should remain simple.

---

# Future Extensions

Possible future additions:

- Pet Traits
- Pet Skills
- Pet Evolutions
- Seasonal Pets
- Limited Pets
- Event Collections

---

# Success Criteria

The Pet System is successful when:

- Players enjoy collecting Pets.
- Different Pet builds remain useful.
- Team switching feels effortless.
- Secret Pets create memorable moments.
- Players continue chasing new Pets throughout progression.

---

# Pet Principle

> Pets are not just collectibles.

> They are the foundation of the player's build.
